Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about trip planning

What documents do I need for international travel?

You'll typically need a valid passport (with at least 6 months validity), visa for your destination country, travel insurance, and any required vaccination certificates. We provide detailed document checklists for each destination.

How far in advance should I plan my trip?

For international trips, we recommend planning 3-6 months in advance to get the best flight and accommodation deals. For popular destinations during peak season, even earlier planning is advisable.

What is your refund policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies that vary by service provider. Most bookings can be cancelled up to 24-48 hours before travel for a full refund. Specific cancellation terms are clearly displayed before you confirm any booking.

Do you provide visa assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive visa guidance including required documents, application procedures, and processing times. For certain destinations, we also offer visa processing services for an additional fee.
